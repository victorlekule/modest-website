Our Story

Going beyond ordinary tours.

Morix Beyond Zanzibar was founded with one simple vision: to create travel experiences that go beyond ordinary tours.

Based in Zanzibar, we combine tourism expertise with creative media to deliver experiences that are personal, authentic, and unforgettable.

Why We Started

Zanzibar and Tanzania are among the most beautiful destinations in the world — yet many visitors only see a small part of their magic. We were created to help travelers explore deeper, travel smarter, and leave with unforgettable stories.
