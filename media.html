    If a specific location requires an entrance or shooting fee, this is covered directly by the client.

    Pickups from remote areas of Zanzibar may attract a small fuel surcharge (stated during booking).

  • Printed Albums

    All packages are strictly digital delivery. Custom physical photo book printing can’t be provided.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get our edited media?

Standard delivery is between 4 to 7 days depending on the package. If you are on a tight schedule, our Premium packages include Priority 72‑hour Express Editing to ensure you get your content before leaving the island.

Do we get the RAW, unedited photos?

To maintain the high aesthetic standard of our Creative Studio, we primarily deliver beautifully color‑graded, fully edited files. However, RAW files can be requested or are included for free under our Elite Premium tiers.

What happens if it rains on the day of our outdoor shoot?

The weather can change, but don't worry! If it rains heavily during your scheduled slot, we will smoothly reschedule your session to the next available sunny slot for free.
