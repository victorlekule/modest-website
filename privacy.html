PRIVACY POLICY | MORIX BEYOND

Your Data Security and Privacy
is Our Utmost Priority

Effective Date: June 01, 2026

Information We Collect

To provide a seamless, premium, and tailor-made travel experience, we collect the following categories of information:

  • Personal Identifiers: Full names, passport details (required strictly for national park permits and domestic flight bookings), nationality, and dietary or accessibility requirements.
  • Contact Information: Email addresses, phone numbers, and WhatsApp contact details.
  • Billing & Financial Data: All online transactions are processed through highly secure, globally accredited third-party payment gateways. Morix Beyond does not store your credit/debit card numbers or CVV codes on our servers.
  • Media & Creative Content: For clients utilizing our Creative Studio, we securely store your edited high-definition photos, videos, and drone footage in private cloud galleries for up to 6 months.

How We Use Your Information

We utilize your personal data strictly to fulfill your high-end travel logistics, including:

  • Securing mandatory government conservation permits and safari park entries.
  • Booking internal private charter or domestic fly-in safari aircraft seats.
  • Communicating instant itinerary updates and providing 24/7 WhatsApp concierge support during your stay.
  • Delivering your private digital media links from the Morix Creative Studio.
  • Complying with local legal frameworks, tourism board regulations, and 18% VAT taxing mandates under the United Republic of Tanzania and the Revolutionary Government of Zanzibar.

Data Protection, Sharing & Security

Zero Data Selling:

Morix Beyond strictly never sells, rents, trades, or leaks your personal information, email addresses, or travel logs to third-party marketing companies.

Trusted Logistics Partners Only:

Your information is shared exclusively with certified operators essential to your trip (e.g., luxury safari lodges, domestic airlines, and government immigration systems where legally required).

Encryption Protocols:

Our website utilizes advanced Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption to protect all input forms, data transmissions, and digital communications from unauthorized access.

International Compliance (GDPR & Global Standards)

We respect global privacy frameworks. No matter where you are booking from—be it the United States, Europe, or beyond—you hold the following rights regarding your data:

  • The Right to Access & Correction: You can request a copy of the personal details we hold for your booking at any time.
  • The Right to Deletion: Upon the successful completion of your African vacation and the safe delivery of your Creative Studio media, you may request the permanent removal of your personal profiles from our active database.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses standard cookies and secure analytical tools (like Google Analytics) to understand visitor traffic, track which safari or island packages are trending, and optimize the user experience. You can easily adjust your web browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer.

Updates to This Policy

As tourism laws, booking technologies, or local Zanzibar insurance mandates evolve, Morix Beyond reserves the right to update this policy. Any modifications will be instantly updated on this page with the revised effective date.
