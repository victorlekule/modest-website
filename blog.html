Mandatory Inbound Travel Insurance for Zanzibar

All visitors traveling to Zanzibar are required to have mandatory inbound travel insurance issued by the Zanzibar authorities before arrival. This is separate from your personal travel insurance and is mandatory for entry.

  • Medical emergencies
  • Accidents & Evacuation
  • Lost or delayed luggage
  • Apply & pay online before arrival
  • Show document at airport/immigration
  • Required for ALL international travelers
Comprehensive Guide

Best Time to Visit Zanzibar for Beach Lovers

Zanzibar is famous for its powder-white beaches, warm turquoise waters, and breathtaking sunsets. Whether you are planning a relaxing holiday, a romantic escape, or a photography trip, choosing the right time to visit can make a big difference in your experience.

Weather Overview

Zanzibar has a tropical climate with warm temperatures throughout the year (25°C - 32°C). The dry seasons are sunny and ideal for activities. The rainy seasons bring short or heavy showers, higher humidity, and rougher sea conditions.

Top Seasons for Beach Activities

June to October

Cooler temperatures, low humidity, clear oceans. Perfect for:

  • Swimming & Snorkeling
  • Sandbank Excursions
  • Sunset Dhow Cruises
  • Drone & Pro Photography

December to February

Hotter, mostly dry and sunny. Warm seas. Perfect for:

  • Escaping Winter
  • Honeymoon Trips
  • Diving & Watersports
  • Romantic Beach Dinners

Months to Avoid (Or Travel With Caution)

March to May (Long Rains): Heavy rain can last for days, rough oceans, cancelled boat trips, and reduced underwater visibility. Good for budget travelers.

November (Short Rains): Unpredictable, short showers. Travel is possible but conditions are less stable.

Best Time for Tanzania Safaris

1

Dry Season (June – October)

The absolute best time for safaris. Animals gather near water sources, roads are dry. Top parks: Serengeti, Ngorongoro, Tarangire, Mikumi.

2

Short Dry Season (Dec – Feb)

Excellent safari period with warm temperatures. Famous for baby animals being born in the Serengeti.

3

Rainy Season (March – May)

Low season. Cheaper travel, lush green parks, but heavy rain makes animal spotting much harder.

Quick Tips

Visa & Entry

Get on arrival or online. Always check current rules.

Currency

USD and TZS widely accepted. Carry small notes.

Local Culture

Muslim-majority. Dress modestly in towns/villages.

Transport

Private transfers are the safest, most reliable way to move.

Best Beaches Guide

  • Kendwa & Nungwi Wide beaches, luxury, parties, perfect sunsets. Best for nightlife & energy.
  • Paje Strong winds, shallow waters. Best for kitesurfing and backpacker vibes.
  • Matemwe Faces Mnemba Island. Best for absolute privacy and snorkeling.
  • Jambiani Fishing villages, long sand stretches. Best for cultural romance.
