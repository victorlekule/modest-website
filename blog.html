Weather & Timing

Best Time to Visit Zanzibar for Beach Lovers

Zanzibar is famous for its white-sand beaches and turquoise waters, but choosing the right time to visit can make your experience even better.

Weather Overview

Zanzibar experiences two rainy seasons. The "long rains" occur in April and May, while the "short rains" happen in November. The dry seasons offer the most predictable beach weather.

Best Months for Beach Activities

For the ultimate experience, aim for June–October or December–February. These months offer clear blue skies and minimal humidity.
