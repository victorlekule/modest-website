The Experience

Feel the rush of the wind and the splash of the Indian Ocean as you captain your own premium jet ski along Zanzibar's most beautiful coastline.

What's Included

  • High‑performance Yamaha Jet Ski.
  • Premium, safety life jackets for both riders.
  • Professional pre‑ride safety briefing and operating instructions.
  • Certified safety guide monitoring from a distance (for your peace of mind).

What's Not Included

Good to Know

  • Perfect For: Content creators, couples, and travelers.
  • Age Limit: Drivers must be 18+ years old. Children can ride as passengers.
  • No Experience Needed: Our instructors will teach you how to ride in less than 3 minutes.
