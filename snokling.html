The Experience

Dive into Zanzibar's premier marine sanctuary. Swim alongside vibrant coral gardens, colorful tropical fish, and experience the magical underwater biosphere.

What's Included

  • 2-3 Hours fully guided boat tour.
  • Premium snorkeling gear (Mask, snorkel, and fins — disinfected before use).
  • Traditional motorized dhow or speed boat transport.
  • Certified local marine guide who knows the best spots to find marine life.
  • Refreshments: Freshly sliced tropical fruits (Mango, pineapple) and unlimited cold drinking water.
  • All marine park conservation entrance fees.

What's Not Included

  • Wetsuit (Usually not needed as water temperature averages 27°C).

Good to Know

  • Swimmers & Non-Swimmers: Safe for everyone. Life jackets and floating rings are provided, and our guide stays in the water with you.
  • Eco-Friendly: We strictly practice "Look but don't touch" to preserve our beautiful coral reefs.
