8-Day Lemosho Route
Scenic & Less Crowded Trek

The Lemosho Route is widely considered the most scenic and rewarding way to climb Mount Kilimanjaro. Starting on the remote western side of the mountain, this route offers breathtaking landscapes, rich wildlife, and excellent acclimatization before joining the Machame Route for the final ascent. It is ideal for climbers seeking high summit success rates with fewer crowds.

Trekking Itinerary

Day 1: Arusha – Londorossi Gate – Mti Mkubwa Camp

Altitude: 2,100m – 2,650m | Distance: 6km | Time: 3-4 hours

Begin your trek through lush rainforest. This area is home to colobus monkeys and unique plant life.

Day 2: Mti Mkubwa – Shira 1 Camp

Altitude: 2,650m – 3,610m | Distance: 8km | Time: 5-6 hours

Day 3: Shira 1 – Shira 2 Camp

Altitude: 3,610m – 3,850m | Distance: 7km | Time: 3-4 hours

Day 4: Shira 2 – Lava Tower – Barranco Camp

Altitude: Up to 4,600m | Distance: 10km | Time: 6-8 hours

Day 5: Barranco – Karanga Camp

Altitude: 3,960m – 4,035m | Distance: 5km | Time: 4-5 hours

Day 6: Karanga – Barafu Camp

Altitude: 4,035m – 4,640m | Distance: 4km | Time: 3-4 hours

Day 7: Summit Day – Barafu to Uhuru Peak – Mweka Camp

Altitude: 4,640m → 5,895m → 3,080m | Distance: 17km | Time: 12-15 hrs

Start the ascent around midnight. Reach Stella Point for sunrise, then continue to Uhuru Peak (5,895 m), the highest point in Africa. Celebrate your achievement before descending to Mweka Camp.

Day 8: Mweka Camp – Mweka Gate – Arusha

Altitude: 3,080m – 1,640m | Distance: 10km | Time: 3-4 hours

7-Day Machame Route
The "Whiskey Route"

Day 3: Shira Camp – Lava Tower – Barranco Camp

Day 7: Mweka Camp – Mweka Gate – Arusha

Altitude: 3,080m → 1,640m | Distance: 10km | Time: 3-4 hours

6-Day Marangu Route
The "Coca-Cola Route"

Climb Africa’s highest mountain via the most established and comfortable trail on Mount Kilimanjaro. The Marangu Route is the only route offering mountain hut accommodation, making it ideal for trekkers who prefer solid shelter, well-defined paths, and a steady ascent profile.

Trekking Itinerary

Day 1: Arusha – Marangu Gate – Mandara Hut

Altitude: 1,860m → 2,700m | Distance: 8km | Time: 4-5 hours

Day 2: Mandara Hut – Horombo Hut

Altitude: 2,700m → 3,720m | Distance: 12km | Time: 6-8 hours

Day 3: Acclimatization Day at Horombo Hut

A crucial day for altitude adaptation. Enjoy a short hike toward Zebra Rocks or Mawenzi Ridge before returning to Horombo Hut.

Day 4: Horombo Hut – Kibo Hut

Altitude: 3,720m → 4,703m | Distance: 10km | Time: 6-7 hours

Day 5: Summit Day – Kibo to Uhuru Peak to Horombo

Altitude: 4,703m → 5,895m → 3,720m | Distance: 21km

Day 6: Horombo Hut – Marangu Gate – Arusha

Altitude: 3,720m → 1,860m | Distance: 20km | Time: 5-7 hours

Package Includes

  • 5 nights’ mountain hut accommodation
  • Pre- and post-trek hotel in Arusha
  • All meals during the trek
  • National Park entrance and rescue fees
